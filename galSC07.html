ESC

The Computer and Information Sciences Department at SC'07

CIS faculty and students have presented our department’s research at SC’07, the International Conference for High Performance Computing, Networking, Storage, and Analysis in Reno, Nevada. Our department shared a research booth with Dr. Gao’s group (ECE). This year SC’07 attracted more than 9,000 computer scientists from industry, research labs, and universities. A lot of people visited our booth and discussed research with our students and our faculty. It was a great success!

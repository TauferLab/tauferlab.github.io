Democratizing Data-Driven Scientific Discovery

NSDF connects scientific data, computing, networks, software, facilities, and people through a federated digital backbone designed to make advanced, AI-enabled research more accessible.

Purpose

Vision, Mission, and Theory of Change

01

Vision

A globally connected research environment where scientific investigation is not limited by the scale, location, or complexity of data.

02

Mission

Democratize access to large-scale scientific data through production-grade solutions for storage, movement, processing, discovery, and analysis across commodity systems, cloud, and HPC.

03

Theory of Change

Connect communities, reusable services, and shared infrastructure so that scientific facilities and researchers can accomplish together what no institution can achieve alone.

Why It Matters

Intellectual Merit

The NSDF pilot established a modular, containerized, and configurable data-fabric testbed that connects data sources with computing and networking resources. The approach addresses the “missing middle” between local research systems and the largest national resources.

Public Benefit

Broader Impacts

NSDF expands access to scientific data and cyberinfrastructure across disciplines and institutions, supports education and workforce development, and enables faster discovery in areas including materials, environment, energy, healthcare, physics, and astronomy.
